# Natural Light — Brand Context

## What they are
Anheuser-Busch value beer brand. "Natty Light" — the definitive college party beer.

## Core identity
- College culture, house parties, tailgates
- Irreverent, self-deprecating humor
- The anti-craft beer — proudly cheap, proudly unpretentious
- Meme-native brand voice

## Key audiences
- College students 21-24 (core)
- Recent grads with nostalgic attachment
- Value beer drinkers
- Greek life, intramural sports, party culture

## Strategic priorities
- Maintain relevance with each new college generation
- Extend beyond "college only" without losing the identity
- Social-first marketing — memes, stunts, creator partnerships

## What makes them interesting for cultural forecasting
- Generational identity marker — "what you drank in college" = cultural signaling
- The premium vs. value tension plays directly to their advantage during economic anxiety
- College culture is shifting (less drinking, more wellness) — existential relevance question
- Meme culture fluency makes them a test case for brand voice in algorithm-mediated spaces
