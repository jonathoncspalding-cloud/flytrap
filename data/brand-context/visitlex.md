# VisitLEX — Brand Context

## What they are
Destination marketing organization for Lexington, Kentucky. They drive tourism and cultural engagement with the city.

## Core identity
- Horse capital of the world (Keeneland, horse farms, bourbon trail)
- College town energy (University of Kentucky)
- Southern hospitality meets creative/culinary scene
- Growing arts, food, and craft beverage culture

## Key audiences
- Weekend travelers (2-4 hour drive radius)
- Bourbon enthusiasts and culinary tourists
- Equine industry visitors
- Young professionals considering relocation

## Strategic priorities
- Position Lexington as more than "horse country" — show the full cultural picture
- Drive shoulder-season visitation (outside peak bourbon/racing seasons)
- Appeal to younger, culturally-curious travelers

## What makes them interesting for cultural forecasting
- Sits at the intersection of Southern identity, craft culture, and college-town progressivism
- Bourbon tourism is a cultural phenomenon, not just a product category
- The "authenticity" tension hits them directly — they ARE the authentic alternative to Nashville/Asheville saturation
