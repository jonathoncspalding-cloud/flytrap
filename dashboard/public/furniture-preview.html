Designer's Notes -- Isabel

1. Emerald Wingback (16x32): Tall, regal silhouette with flared wings and diamond tufting. The emerald palette brings LIFE to any corner. Those turned wooden legs add warmth. Place next to a bookshelf for an instant reading nook -- magnifique!

2. Burnt Orange Mid-Century (16x32): Low-slung, sleek, with those signature splayed tapered legs that angle outward -- pure 1960s. The warm terracotta will POP against the cool wood floors. Pair with a plant for that Palm Springs lounge vibe.

3. Sapphire Reading Chair (32x32): Oversized, jewel-toned LUXURY. Gold nailhead trim along the arms, diamond tufting on the tall back, bun feet. This is the throne of someone who takes their reading VERY seriously. The sapphire blue creates stunning contrast with any warm wood.

4. Oxblood Club Chair (32x32): Massive rolled arms, brass stud detailing everywhere, that gorgeous stitching line across the back. The worn leather highlights give it CHARACTER -- like it's been in a private club since 1923. Bellissimo!

Tell me which ones you ADORE, which need tweaking, and which shall be banished. I will place the chosen ones in our palazzo with the reverence they deserve!
